## Overview

Leadership coaching helps entrepreneurs, executives, and professionals strengthen their ability to guide teams, make strategic decisions, and maintain discipline during complex business challenges.

Many leadership coaching frameworks focus on developing:

- mindset and mental resilience  
- strategic thinking and decision making  
- communication and leadership influence  
- consistent high-performance habits  
- long-term vision and organizational leadership  

The resources referenced in this repository explore these areas from an educational perspective.
